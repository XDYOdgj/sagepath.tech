HAOCHEN Fridge Water Line Kit

Premium quality stainless steel braided hose with Tee Stop Valve for Ice Maker and Refrigerator Water Systems

Potable Drinking Water Use

Our ice maker hose is constructed with BPA-free materials, featuring a food-grade PEX inner tube that ensures safe, clean, and contaminant-free water for your family. You can rest assured that every ice cube and water flow is pure, odorless, and tasteless, providing a better drinking experience.

Multiple Applications & Compatibility

Designed with universal 1/4" fittings, this water line is ideal for connection to existing angle stop valves or other 1/4" outlets for ice makers, refrigerators, RO water systems, and drinking water faucets. It is compatible with leading brands such as Samsung, GE, LG, Whirlpool, and Frigidaire, making it a versatile solution for your kitchen needs.

Easy Installation & Leak-Free Performance

With brass compression fittings on both ends and a built-in sealing ring, this hose provides a secure and leak-proof connection. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or first-time user, installation or replacement is quick and straightforward, taking just minutes to complete.

Rust-Proof and Pressure-Resistant Design

Crafted with a durable 304 stainless steel braided outer layer, our water hose offers excellent resistance to rust and high pressure. This ensures long-lasting performance even in moisture-prone environments, offering reliability and peace of mind.

Comprehensive Kit & Warranty

This ice maker installation kit includes a 3/8"-1/4"-3/8" Tee Stop Valve, which allows you to create a branch in your existing water supply and control water flow with ease. A detailed instruction manual is also provided, ensuring a smooth setup process. Enjoy a 5-year warranty on the drinking water hose for lasting quality and support.
