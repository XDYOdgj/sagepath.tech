Maxblue W10295370A Replacement for Everydrop Filter 1 - Premium Refrigerator Water Filter

Ensure your family drinks safe, pure, and great-tasting water with this high-quality replacement refrigerator water filter from Maxblue. Perfectly designed for a range of models, including Whirlpool and Kenmore.

Why Choose Maxblue?

Maxblue water filters are built to deliver superior filtration performance, with a focus on your health and safety. Our premium carbon block technology is NSF 42 and NSF 372 certified, meaning it's safe for use and free from harmful lead content.

Advanced Filtration System

Efficiently removes a wide range of contaminants from your water, including heavy metals, chlorine, unpleasant odors, THMs, VOCs, particles, and more. Independent laboratory testing ensures optimal performance with every use.

Perfect Fit

Engineered to fit multiple refrigerator models, including Whirlpool EDR1RXD1, EDR1RXD1B, P8RFWB2L, P4RFWB, Kenmore 46-9081, 46-9930, and others. No water leakage issues when installed correctly.

Premium Filter Material

Made from 100% selected coconut shell carbon block, offering a larger surface area and more micropores than standard carbon filters. This results in improved adsorption and better-tasting water.

Easy Maintenance

Includes month install stickers to keep track of when it's time to replace the filter. Whether it's 6 months or 200 gallons, these stickers help you maintain consistent water quality.

Quality Assurance

Every component, including the filter itself, undergoes rigorous testing to meet high industry standards. Maxblue stands behind the quality of its products and ensures reliability from start to finish.
