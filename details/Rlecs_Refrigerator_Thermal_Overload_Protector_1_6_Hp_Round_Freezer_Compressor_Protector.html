RLECS Refrigerator Thermal Overload Protector - 1/6 HP (125W)

This durable and reliable overload protector is designed for use in small refrigeration units such as car refrigerators, portable freezers, and beverage or wine coolers. Perfect for replacing worn-out parts, it ensures your cooling system runs efficiently and safely.

Key Features

  • Compatible with 1/6 HP (125W) compressors
  • Designed for round freezers and similar appliances
  • Helps protect compressors from overheating and excessive current
  • Easy to install and saves both time and money
  • Helps maintain the freshness of food, wine, and beverages

Why Choose This Protector?

By using this thermal overload protector, you can avoid expensive compressor replacements and unexpected breakdowns. It's an essential accessory for anyone looking to keep their cooling appliances in top condition without compromising performance.

How to Choose the Right Protector

When selecting an overload protector, ensure that the original electrical parameters match the replaced parts. Always verify the compressor power and make sure it does not exceed the protector's capacity for safe and effective operation.
