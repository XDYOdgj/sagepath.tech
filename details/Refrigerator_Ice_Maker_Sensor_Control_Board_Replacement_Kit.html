This high-quality sensor control board kit is designed to replace multiple part numbers including 2198585, 2198586, 2220398, 2220402, 2255114, 4388635, and 4389102R. It is compatible with major refrigerator brands like Whirlpool and Kenmore, and fits models such as W10193666, W10193840, W10290817, and W10757851.

Key Features

  • Precision temperature and ice level control for optimal performance
  • Built-in infrared sensor to detect ice level accurately
  • Designed for durability and long-term use
  • Easy to install with clear instructions included

Why Choose This Product?

Upgrade your refrigerator's ice maker with this reliable sensor control board kit. It ensures consistent ice production and improves energy efficiency, helping you save on electricity costs and reduce your environmental impact. The infrared technology makes it easy for the ice maker to detect when the bucket is full, stopping production automatically to avoid overfilling and wastage.

Before installation, always disconnect the power supply and wear protective gloves. This ensures your safety during the replacement process. The installation typically takes just a few minutes and requires no special tools, making it an ideal solution for both DIYers and professional technicians.
