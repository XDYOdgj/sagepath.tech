ZNTVW 241688701 Door Seal Flap - Genuine Refrigerator Replacement Part

Effortlessly repair your fridge with this reliable door seal flap designed for Frigidaire and Electrolux refrigerator models.

Why You Need This Door Seal Flap

A faulty refrigerator door seal can lead to inefficient cooling and excessive frost buildup in your fridge. This genuine seal flap is the perfect solution to ensure a proper fit and airtight closure, helping you save energy and maintain optimal fridge performance. It's an essential part for preserving the freshness of your food and keeping your appliance running smoothly.

Wide Compatibility

This door seal flap is compatible with a variety of cooler models including BFHS2611LM0, BFHS2611LM1, BFHS2611LM2, CRSE230KW0, CRSE234FB0, CRSE234FB1, CRSE234FB2, CRSE234FB3, CRSE234FB4, CRSE234FQ0, CRSE234FQ1, CRSE234FQ2, CRSE234FQ3, CRSE234FQ4, CRSE234FSM0, CRSE234FSM1, CRSE234FSM2, CRSE234FSM3, CRSE234FSM4, CRSE234FW0, CRSE234FW1, CRSE234FW2, CRSE234FW3, CRSE234FW4. Perfect for replacing your worn-out seal with ease.

Easy Installation Process

Replacing your fridge door seal has never been simpler. Simply follow these steps to install it quickly and effectively:

  • Disconnect the power to the refrigerator
  • Remove the escutcheon carefully
  • Disconnect the harness at the bond
  • Take out the old assembly
  • Install the new door seal flap
With minimal effort, you'll have your refrigerator sealed and working efficiently again.
