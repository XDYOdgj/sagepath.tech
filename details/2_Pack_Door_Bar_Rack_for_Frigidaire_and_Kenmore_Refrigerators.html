2-Pack Door Bar Rack for Frigidaire and Kenmore Refrigerators

Enhance your kitchen organization with this durable 2-pack door bar rack. Designed to fit a variety of refrigerators, including models from Frigidaire, Kenmore, Electrolux, Kelvinator, Sears, Tappan, and White-Westinghouse, this accessory is perfect for maximizing your fridge's storage space. Whether you're looking to keep snacks, drinks, or small items within easy reach, this bar rack is built for reliability and ease of use.

Each rack measures 1-3/4" in height and 5-7/8" in depth, offering a secure and stable hold for your essentials. The maximum length of 24-3/4" ensures that you can place it on the door without compromising functionality. Made with quality materials, these racks are easy to install and maintain, making them a great addition to your appliance setup.

It's important to note that this specific model is different from 240534901, which features a 4.5" depth, and 240338001, which has a guard height of 2-1/2". Before purchasing, make sure to confirm compatibility with your refrigerator model. Works with popular appliances like Frigidaire CRTE181AW1, CRTE181AW2, CRTE181AW3, CRTE181AW4 and Kenmore CRTE181AW1, CRTE181AW2, CRTE181AW3, CRTE181AW4.

Whether you're replacing a damaged part or simply upgrading your fridge's storage solution, these door bar racks are an excellent choice. They combine practicality with a sleek design to fit seamlessly into your kitchen. Perfect for both home and commercial use, these racks are built to last and provide great value for your money.
