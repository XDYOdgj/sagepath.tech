Electric Stove Burner Covers - Set of 4

Bring a touch of rustic charm to your kitchen with the Highland Cow Round Stove Burner Covers. Available in 8 inches and 10 inches sizes, this set is designed to enhance both functionality and style.

Durable & Easy to Clean

These burner covers are crafted from high-quality metal, making them tough enough for daily use and easy to maintain. Simply hand wash with a damp cloth, wipe clean with a soapy cloth, and dry with a towel to keep your stove looking as good as new.

Functional Design

Designed to cover the burners on your stove top when not in use, these covers protect the burners from spills and splashes, keeping your cooktop clean and your kitchen organized. They also help to increase your usable counter space, making your kitchen more efficient.

Stylish Kitchen Decor

With their uniquely styled, autumn-themed design, these covers add a warm and inviting touch to any farmhouse kitchen. Whether you're cooking for your family or entertaining guests, they elevate the look of your appliances and create a more welcoming cooking environment.

Versatile & Practical

Perfect for both electric and gas stoves, this set of 4 covers includes 2 packs of 8-inch and 10-inch covers, each with a height of 0.82 inches. They are an ideal addition to any kitchen, providing both protection and aesthetic appeal with minimal effort.
