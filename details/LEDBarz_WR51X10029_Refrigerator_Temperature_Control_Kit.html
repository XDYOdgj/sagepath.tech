LEDBarz WR51X10029 Refrigerator Temperature Control Kit

Ensure your refrigerator runs smoothly and efficiently with the LEDBarz temperature control kit. Designed for GE, Hotpoint, and Kenmore models, this kit provides reliable temperature regulation to keep your food fresh and your energy bills in check.

Why You Need This Kit

Modern refrigerators rely on precise temperature management to maintain food preservation and energy efficiency. The LEDBarz WR51X10029 control kit is engineered with advanced technology that ensures your appliances maintain a consistent and optimal cooling environment.

Built for Durability and Performance

Constructed with a high-quality 18-gauge stainless steel wiring harness, this kit is built to last. It resists corrosion and high-temperature fluctuations, ensuring reliable signal transmission between components and reducing the risk of system failures.

Easy Installation Made Simple

Designed for straightforward installation, the LEDBarz kit features color-coded connectors and universal mounting brackets. No soldering or special tools are required, making it ideal for both DIY enthusiasts and professional technicians.

Protect Your Investment

This essential part addresses common refrigerator issues such as compressor overcycling, evaporator coil freezing, and temperature inconsistencies. By stabilizing your fridge's performance, it helps extend the appliance's lifespan and reduces the need for costly repairs.

Compatible with Multiple Models

Whether you're repairing a GE, Hotpoint, or Kenmore refrigerator, the LEDBarz WR51X10029 kit is the perfect upgrade. It works with a range of OEM codes including AH303760, PS303760, 880031, and more, ensuring broad applicability and peace of mind.

Upgrade your refrigerator's performance with a reliable, easy-to-install temperature control solution from LEDBarz. Keep your food safe, your energy usage low, and your appliance running smoothly for years to come.
