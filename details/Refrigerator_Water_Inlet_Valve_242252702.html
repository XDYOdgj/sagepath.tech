GREALTH Refrigerator Water Inlet Valve - Triple Solenoid Assembly

A reliable and durable replacement for your Frigidaire, Kenmore, Electrolux, or Crosley fridge. Perfect for fixing water dispenser or ice maker issues and ensuring smooth water flow throughout your appliance.

Product Overview

This GREALTH refrigerator water inlet valve assembly features a triple solenoid design and is engineered to provide long-lasting performance. It is designed to be a direct replacement for multiple part numbers including 240321801, 240505101, 240505102, 240508101, 240508102, 240531101, 241734301, 241734302, 7241734301, 2692163, AP5671757, and PS7784018. Choose the right replacement for your fridge with confidence.

Compatible Brands

This water inlet valve is compatible with leading refrigerator brands such as Frigidaire, Kenmore, Electrolux, and Crosley. It is also suitable for models from Gibson and WhiteWestinghouse. If you're unsure if it fits your refrigerator, feel free to contact us with your model number for verification.

Built for Durability

Constructed from high-quality ABS plastic and stainless steel, this valve is resistant to rust and breakage, ensuring it lasts for years. Its robust design effectively manages water flow, eliminating common issues like no water flow, water leaks, or ice maker malfunction.

Easy Installation

Designed for straightforward installation, this valve works with screw-type fittings. To replace, cut off the old screw with a razor blade (ensure a straight cut), insert the water tube until it bottoms out in the connector (approximately 1/2 inch). For removal, simply depress the white collet to release the water line.
