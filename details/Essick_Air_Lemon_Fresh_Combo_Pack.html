Essick Air Lemon Fresh Humidifier Fragrance and Bacteriostat Combo Pack

Elevate your indoor air quality with this essential combo pack for Essick Air humidifiers. Designed for use with your humidifier, this set includes a fresh lemon fragrance and a bacteriostatic treatment that helps keep your air clean and pleasant.

Enhance Your Environment

This fragrance and treatment combo is perfect for those who want to maintain a fresh, clean-smelling atmosphere in their home or office. The lemon scent adds a refreshing aroma to your humidifier, while the bacteriostatic treatment helps prevent the growth of bacteria in the water reservoir, ensuring a healthier environment for you and your family.

Easy to Use & Customize

Designed for simplicity, this combo pack provides clear guidelines for optimal usage. For every gallon of water, use 3 capfuls of the lemon fragrance to achieve your desired level of freshness. The bacteriostatic treatment should be used at a rate of approximately 1/6 fluid ounce (about 1/8 capful) per gallon of water. This allows for easy adjustment based on your personal preferences and the size of your humidifier.

Durable & Reliable

Made in the USA, this combo pack is built for long-lasting performance. The 24 oz fragrance bottle and the 32 oz bacteriostatic bottle are both high-quality, ensuring consistent results with every use. Ideal for regular use in home or office humidifiers, this set is a must-have for anyone looking to improve their indoor air experience.

Create a Cleaner, Fresher Atmosphere

Whether you're using your humidifier for health benefits or to refresh your living space, the Lemon Fresh combo pack delivers a pleasant and healthy solution. Enhance the air in your room with the crisp scent of lemon, and keep your humidifier running smoothly with effective bacterial control.
