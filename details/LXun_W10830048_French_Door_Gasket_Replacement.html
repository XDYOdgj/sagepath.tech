Why Upgrade Your Refrigerator Door Gasket?

Over time, old refrigerator gaskets can develop cracks, lose flexibility, and fail to create a proper seal. This can lead to cold air leaks, increased energy consumption, and even food spoilage. The LXun upgraded gasket is designed to provide a reliable seal, keeping your fridge cool and your food fresh for longer. It's an ideal solution for Maytag and Kenmore models requiring a durable and resilient gasket.

Compatibility

This refrigerator door gasket is compatible with Maytag and Kenmore models including W10830048, W10179334, W10443240, and others. It is specifically designed for the left side of French door refrigerators, ensuring a proper fit without any gaps or uneven sealing.

Installation Tips

Follow these simple steps for a smooth installation:

  1. Warm the gasket slightly using a hair dryer to remove any kinks and creases.
  2. Check that the gasket is flat and ready for installation.
  3. Remove the old gasket and carefully align the new one.
  4. Secure the four corners and gradually press the sides to ensure a tight, even fit.
  5. Close the refrigerator door and use a hair dryer to flatten any remaining creases.
  6. Optional: Use tape to hold the door closed for 24 hours to ensure a proper seal.
