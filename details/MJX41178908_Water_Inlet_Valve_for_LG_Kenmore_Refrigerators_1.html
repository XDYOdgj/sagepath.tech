When to Replace Your Water Inlet Valve?

Consider upgrading your water inlet valve if your refrigerator is experiencing:

  • No water entering the fridge despite the unit operating.
  • Water leaking from the refrigerator.
  • Small or misshaped ice cubes from the icemaker.
  • Non-functioning water dispenser.
  • Issues with the water freezing or dispensing smoothly.
