CCTP WR55X26671 Light Board LED

Enhance the lighting inside your GE refrigerator or freezer with the CCTP WR55X26671 LED Light Board. Designed to fit seamlessly behind the light lens, this modern LED board delivers bright, even illumination to keep your food and items clearly visible. Perfect for replacing the original PS11767930, AP6035586, or other compatible parts like 4468532, EAP11767930, and LED3344588.

Compatibility

This product is compatible with a variety of GE refrigerator models including: GZS22IMNPHES, XSS25GSHLCSS, GSS25GGHKCBB, DSE25JSHGCSS, GSS23GGKJCBB, GSS25IGNPHBB, PSE25KGHEHWW, GSE25GGHJCBB, PZS22MSKGHSS, GSS23HGHJCBB, GSE23GGKNCBB, GSS23HMHJCES, GSS23GMKJCES, GSS25IMNPHES, GSS25IGNEHBB, GSE25GSHRCSS, GSE23GGKRCWW, DSE25JMHECES, GSS25IYNBHFS, GZS22DSJPFSS, DSE25JEMKCDS, XSS23GGKRCWW, GSE25GGHJCCC, GSE25GGHNCWW, GSE25GGHPCBB, XSS23GGKBCWW, GSE25GSHMCSS, GSS25IMNEHES, XSS25GGHNCWW, GSS25GGHNCWW, GZS22DGJRFBB, GSS23GGKPCBB, and GSS25GMHLCES.

Installation Tips

For safe and effective installation, ensure the refrigerator is unplugged before proceeding. Store any perishable or sensitive food items in a cool place while the power is off. We recommend wearing work gloves to protect your hands during the process.
