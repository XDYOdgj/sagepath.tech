Enhance the look and functionality of your RV or camper with our durable 4-piece black window and vent knobs. These modern, user-friendly knobs are designed to fit most standard RV windows and vents, offering a sleek and reliable solution for your recreational vehicle.

Each knob features a 2-1/4 inch diameter plastic handle with a 1-inch shaft, making them ideal for use with various RV window and vent systems. The center hole is precisely sized at 7/16 inch to ensure a perfect fit for most residential and commercial trailer windows.

Built to Last
